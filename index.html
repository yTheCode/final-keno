About Us

KENO FITNESS CENTER is a space where you can achieve your fitness goals whatever your skill levels are, was built and is managed by Ken Steven Tan Lao.

Ken has been a long time fitness enthusiast. Starting when he was still in college, he would be a regular client of a local fitness center. Here, he was inspired to share the joy of fitness to everyone, making him start his own.

Observing that in San Jose, gyms were non-existent, he decided to build KENO Fitness Center in 2017 to provide the local residents a facility to help them reach their fitness goals.
